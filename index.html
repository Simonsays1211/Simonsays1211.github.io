How to make a delicious Shin Ramen

Welcome to my site "How to make a delicious Shin Ramen". In this site, we will learn how to make a delicious Shin Ramen. However, You should be old enough to boil water and use knife to cut things.

People need to know how to make Shin Ramen because people can make it easily when they are hungry and they are very tasty. Also, making Shin Ramen takes about 6 minutes to make it. So, people need to know how to make a delicious Shin Ramen.

Materials

  • 500mL of water
  • Egg
  • Pot
  • Shin Ramen
  • Timer
  • Welsh onion
  • rice

Steps

Step 1

pour the water in the pot and boil the water.

Step 2

When the water boils, put in the noodle, Shin Ramen Powder and Welish onion together at once and set the timer to 4 minutes

Step 3

When the timer finishes, put in the egg and boil it for 1 minute and 30 seconds more

Step 4

Enjoy the Ramen :)

Steps 5

Put in rice and eat it
